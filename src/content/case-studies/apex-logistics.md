## The Problem

Apex Logistics' operations team was spending over eight hours every week pulling data from multiple systems, formatting spreadsheets, and compiling reports by hand. The process was tedious, error-prone, and pulled skilled people away from work that actually moved the business forward.

By the time reports reached decision-makers, the data was often already a day or two old. The team knew they were wasting time, but every attempt to speed things up just meant more spreadsheets and more manual steps.

## The Approach

We started by mapping out exactly which reports mattered and where the data lived. Three reports accounted for the bulk of the weekly time sink, so we focused there first.

Zero Fluff built automated data pipelines that pulled directly from Apex's existing systems, transformed the data into the formats the team actually needed, and delivered finished reports on a set schedule. No new platforms to learn, no complex dashboards to maintain -- just the same reports, produced automatically.

## The Outcome

**Eight hours per week freed up** for the operations team. Reports that used to take a full day now generate automatically before the team even arrives on Monday morning.

The data is fresher, the formatting is consistent, and the operations lead can spend that reclaimed time on the strategic work they were hired to do. As their operations director put it: "We finally stopped drowning in spreadsheets."
